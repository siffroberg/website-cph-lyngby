Project description

For the exam project, the task was to develop a website which forms a portfolio,
starting from what I have learned in the first semester.
The portfolio should contain previous projects and updates of what I have learned during the year. Project planning,
justifications and explanations had to be available.
Furthermore, a video had to be produced for the site.

Project management

My work started with doing project management, so I had a full overview of the scope of the task and time consumption.
This started with a PBS, which describes what my portfolio should consist of.
In addition, I created a WBS, which outlined the work tasks,

My idea

The idea was that the website should exude personality and give the user the feeling of me right away.
The front page had to give the user a clear idea of ​​what kind of site this is,
and then with an atmospheric background which supported the color choice. The problem was just that,
I didn't know what would radiate me and give the user a sense of who I am as a person.
For a long time I didn't know what my website should look like. I later had to admit that with my current skills I could not bring my idea to life at this time.
Then I had to come up with a whole new alternative. still trying to make it me, but in a more simple way.

Original Design

This was originally what i had in mind, leaning towards a more toned down feel

Style/Inspo

I wanted the user to want to scroll down because it was exciting
and wanted to see more about me and my work.
Ive only used two colours trhoughout my portfolio, black and white because my original design also didnt have many colours.
the only thing that appears to have colour are the social areal. I made the choise to make a hover effect. making it a box hover effect,
so when you put your mouse over it, it will come forward with this red color, as i found it fitting for the different icons
as it was the best match for me, and also trying in someway to stay true to my original design
As for my inspiration I, i looked through pinterest and found lots of
good templates i wanted to recreate or at least work around, but like i said earlier on i had to come to term with
my skills was not yet there. All though it wasnt an easy process I have learned a lot, and gotten a lot more knowledge on the subject
for that I am greatfull

Even though it is as simple as it gets, i still tried to make it appear put together trhoughout the whole site.
However, the appearance of the boxes had to repeat itself throughout the site, to give recognition
and not make it all too messy and unpredictable. As we know it from the Gestalt laws of equality, and symmetry and order.
As for the font, i chose to use cursive and the headings still cursive but making them bold so they would stand out even more from the rest of the text.

Coding

I honestly found the coding really difficult, it surtenly didnt come easy.
I have learned a lot by making mistakes, and learning to see for myself the mistakes that i made.
It has made me faster at realizing my mistakes and how to fix them.
In my overall coding I have used display-flex to define my containers and given them the right value to fit my exact liking
as well as giving my boxes a nice round edge using border-radius. so it would give them a softer look.
I also used it for my gallery part.
i have used align, justify-content as well as padding and
margin-left and right to create my layout as best as I could.
I wanted it to be as simple as possible. thats why my color-scheme is black and white.
and only using colours where i felt it would fit in, as for an eksempel in my photo-gallery
the colour of the font i used rgb(188, 81, 155), as well as using the colour rgb(131, 27, 27) on my effect for the social section.
